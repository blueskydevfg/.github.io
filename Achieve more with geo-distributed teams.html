How I Can Help You Achieve More with Geo-Distributed Teams

Scaling your software organization with nearshore or offshore teams can open doors to larger, cost-effective talent pool which can help your organization achieve faster growth, and greater efficiency. However working cross-culturally and cross-location comes with its own challenges. You might have concerns about maintaining productivity, aligning people with your empowered nordic culture and that teams in other locations are just executing the simpler tasks and not truly integrated and innovating alongside you.

These concerns are valid, and I can help you and your team tackle them.

I bring 20+ years of experience building and leading distributed teams across Europe and Asia. I can help your team improve workflows to connect people across locations, improve collaboration, and ensure your teams are not only productive but deeply engaged. I specialize in transforming teams and processes, improving cross-location collaboration, and aligning distributed teams with business goals. I ensure minimal disruption while delivering long-term improvements in team engagement, productivity, and knowledge-sharing.

Let's work together to make your geo-distributed teams achieve more and your organization scale better.
